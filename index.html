Gunmetal

it's probably the best thing ever

di for people who don't hate themselves

Spring has taken approximately 8 months of my life by forcing me to help coworkers debug the proxy magic and XML spaghetti. Dagger takes too long to compile and is too rigid in the wrong ways for the server side. Guice has become a bloated, visionless mess. Gunmetal has none of these problems! (instead it probably has many other issues that could be harped on...)

Gunmetal is built around the principles of traceability, visibility, type-safety, fast build-deploy, a full set of the most killer DI features, and eschewing non-killer DI features.
